Abun d'Bashmayo

Transliteration

Abun d'bashmayo, nethqadash shmokh.
Tethe malkuthokh.
Nehwe sebyonokh aykano d'bashmayo af b'ar'a.
Hav lan lahma d'sunqonan yawmono.
Washboq lan hawbayn aykano d'af hnan shbaqan l'hayobayn.
Wela ta'lan l'nesyuna.
Ela patzan min bisha.
Metol dilokhie malkutho wahaylo wateshbuhto l'alam almin. Amin.

English Meaning

Our Father in heaven, hallowed be Your name.
Your kingdom come.
Your will be done, on earth as it is in heaven.
Give us today our daily bread.
Forgive us our debts, as we also forgive our debtors.
Lead us not into temptation,
but deliver us from evil.
For Yours is the kingdom, the power and the glory forever. Amen.
