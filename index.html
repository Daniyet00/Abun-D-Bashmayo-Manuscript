Abun d'Bashmayo

Transliteration

Abun d'bašmayo, neṯqadašh šhmokh.
Tiṯhe malkuṯokh.
Nehwe sebyonokh aykano d'bašmayo of b'ar'o.
Hab lan lahmo d'sunqonan yawmono.
Wašbuq lan hawbayn aykano d'of hnan sbaqan l'hayobayn.
Wlo ta'lan l'nesyuno.
Elo fasolan men bišo.
Metul dilokhi malkuṯho haylo wtešbuhto l'olam olmin. Amin.

English Meaning

Our Father in heaven, hallowed be Your name.
Your kingdom come.
Your will be done, on earth as it is in heaven.
Give us today our daily bread.
Forgive us our debts, as we also forgive our debtors.
Lead us not into temptation,
but deliver us from evil.
For Yours is the kingdom, the power and the glory forever. Amin.

About This Project

This website was designed by Daniyel Töre for the Digital Manuscript course. The Syriac prayer "Abun d'Bašhmayo" holds a central place in daily spiritual and liturgical life. It is one of the most widely used and meaningful prayers in the Syriac tradition, which is why it was chosen as the focus of this digital manuscript project.
